# What is Open Truly Chat?

Open Truly Chat is an open-source, self-hosted bot for WhatsApp that automatically replies to your messages in your unique style.

## What does it do?
- Learns how you text by analyzing your exported WhatsApp chat history.
- Lets you mark your closest person’s chat for extra style accuracy.
- Connects to your WhatsApp using a QR code (via whatsapp-web.js).
- Replies to private chats for you, so friends feel you’re still around—even when you’re offline or busy.
- Keeps everything local and private; your data stays with you.

## How does it work?
1. You upload your WhatsApp chat history (.txt files) in the web dashboard.
2. You set your OpenAI API key (for smart replies).
3. You scan a QR code to link your WhatsApp account.
4. The bot starts replying to incoming messages in your style, using AI.

## Why use it?
- Stay connected when you’re offline, traveling, or just want to keep your presence.
- Make your replies feel authentic, not generic.
- All processing happens on your own server—no cloud, no leaks.

## Tech Used
- WhatsApp integration: whatsapp-web.js
- AI replies: OpenAI (GPT)
- Web dashboard: Node.js, React, Vite
